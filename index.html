⚡ Lesson 1: The Golden Rule of DC Electricity

Volts ÷ Ohms = Amps

( A = V / R )

VOLT
The Engine (Push)
OHM
The Squeeze (Restriction)
AMP
The Result (Flow)

Remember: You can add pressure/power (Volts), you can add restrictors (Ohms), but you can't add Amps. They are just the result of the push meeting the squeeze!
