⚡ Lesson 1

The Golden Rule
of DC Electricity

Volts ÷ Ohms = Amps
This one rule powers every circuit ever built.
⚡ Volt — The Push

Electricity needs a push to move.
That push is called Voltage.
No Volt? Nothing moves. Nothing works.

🔮 Ohm — The Squeeze

The wire pushes back against Volt.
That pushback is called Resistance.
More Ohm = less gets through.

💧 Amp — The Result

What makes it through is called Current.
Amp doesn't decide anything.
Amp is always the answer.

That relationship has a name — Ohm's Law.
Control the push. Control the squeeze.
The current is what you get.

Volts ÷ Ohms = Amps

📋 Quick Review

Volt is the push. No Volt — nothing happens.

Ohm is the squeeze. More Ohm means less gets through.

Amp is the result. Amp just does the math.
